Genetic Resources and Uses Symposium

Topics

Breeding, genebank management, and genetic resources, Genetic resources in agriculture, Genetic resource conservation, Exploration on genetic resource, Genebank management, Characterization of genetic resources, Evaluation of genetic resources, Genetic resource transfer, Genetic diversity, Pre-breeding, Genetic material (plant, animal, microbial, or other origin) of actual or potential value, Plants and animals breeding, Uses of genetic resources.

Publication

The accepted and presented papers will be published in the AIP Conferences Proceedings.

Submission

If you want to submit paper, please kindly submit your paper through e-mail: piat@ugm.ac.id

Last full paper submission: September 10th, 2018
