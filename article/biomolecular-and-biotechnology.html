Biomolecular and Biotechnology Symposium

Topics

The symposium topics include among others, breakthroughs in biotechnology and biology molecular research including biocatalysts, genomics, metagenomics, proteomics, metabolomics, bioinformatics and molecular physiology with the recent advances in knowledge and new technologies, have had significant impacts on innovative solutions related to molecular biology, biochemical, environment, bio-industries and plant biotechnology.
